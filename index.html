Winnie the Pooh Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four Winnie the Pooh Characters below. By clicking on a character, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of the crstal is hidden from you until you clock on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of the Characters.
